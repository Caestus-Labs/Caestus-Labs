02 — The Problem

Touch is
completely missing.

Current VR lacks tactile feedback. Existing haptic devices are bulky and imprecise. Developers struggle with complex integration. We're solving all three.

03 — Inside the hardware

Twelve servos.
One system.

A carbon exoskeleton routes force across shoulder, elbow, and wrist. 184g per arm, <4ms latency, cable-driven so the actuators never touch your skin.

04 — Presence

The world
pushes back.

Move your cursor — the environment responds. Every object has weight, every surface resists. This is what training, surgical sim, and remote work feel like when space becomes physical.
